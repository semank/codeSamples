I'm a designer. I make pretty things. & I'm for hire.

Just who is this girl? And why would she say something like that?

I'm a girl with a love for the avant garde, the kooky, and the just plain insane. Pair that with an eye for detail, an artistic streak, and a couple of $10k+ pieces of paper, and you have the recipe for an amazing designer.

I've changed career aspirations about as much as I've changed my underwear. Everything from meteorology to veterinary medicine, to my personal favorite, being a cloud. (I was 5. Don't judge me.) but somehow, I settled on design around high school. The web aspect didn't come around until college, and honestly? I added the major because I enjoyed Flash.

If you're in metro Detroit, you'll probably find me hanging out around Lake Saint Clair, most likely with my old school Minolta in hand, laying in the grass and watching the clouds go by, or simply cruising around town in my bright pink car. You can't miss me. Feel free to say hi!
