Hi there!

My name's Kim. Or Kimberly Marie Seman, if you want to get technical. I'm a 23 year old recent graduate of Macomb Community College, with 2 degrees in Media and Communication Arts: Digital Design and Layout and Interactive Web Media.

I live in a suburb of Detroit, MI with my cats, Bianca and Cici. When I'm not buried neck deep in 10,000 lines of code, I enjoy baking, sewing, and photography. I have a strange obsession with things like Hello Kitty and My Little Pony, as well as the smell of Gain, Starbucks frappuccinos, and nail polish. Seriously, I own over 100 bottles.

More interested in me as a designer than as a person? Check out my resume and my portfolio for my body of work and experience, and feel free to drop me a message via the contact page! I look forward to hearing from you.
